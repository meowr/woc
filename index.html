Wedding & Reception

where

Join us at the Golden Gate Club in the Presidio of San Francisco.

Parking is available, but we encourage you to carpool or call a cab. There will be a shuttle to give you a lift to downtown area hotels at the end of the night.

Both our ceremony and reception will be indoors, but you will want to bring a warm jacket, as it can get chilly in the evenings.

when

The ceremony begins at 5pm. Please plan to arrive at least 20 minutes before it begins.

It will be followed by cocktails, dinner and dancing, also at the Golden Gate Club.
